NEON DEFENCE

Game Rules

Goal:

  • Protect 6 cities from missile attacks

How to Play:

  • Use mouse to aim
  • Click to shoot defensive missiles
  • Intercept enemy missiles before they hit your cities

Waves:

  • 9 waves total
  • Missiles per wave: 10 → 12 → 15 → 18 → 20 → 25 → 30 → 35 → 40
  • Each wave gets 20% faster
  • Missiles launch every 2 seconds

Game Over:

  • When all cities are destroyed

Difficulty:

  • Each wave gets faster
  • Destroyed cities make surviving ones more likely to be targeted

Bonus:

  • Collect power-ups for temporary advantages
  • Higher scores for saving cities and destroying missiles
